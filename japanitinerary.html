Where to Get JR Passes and Important Cards

If you are looking for JR Passes or other important travel cards, you can visit travel agencies or designated JR Pass exchange offices in major Japanese cities. Some options include:

  • Travel agencies in your home country before arriving in Japan
  • JR Pass exchange offices at major airports
  • Designated JR Pass exchange counters in cities like Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto
